🏦 Access to finance

Credit Guarantee

Collateral-free lending through the National Credit Guarantee Scheme.Administered in partnership with the National Bank of Ethiopia.

$7000/once

Supervised with the National Bank of Ethiopia

Learn more

What is included:

  • Loan guarantee coverage for designated startups
  • Reduced collateral requirements
  • Access to participating commercial banks
  • Working-capital and investment financing
  • Guidance on application and eligibility

🔥 Market access

Procurement Preference

Priority access to business-to-government public procurement opportunities across ministries.

$399/month

Business-to-government (B2G) market access

Learn more

What is included:

  • Preference in eligible public tenders
  • Set-aside government contracts
  • Simplified bidding for designated startups
  • Access to ministry procurement pipelines
  • Support through the procurement process
Need more info? Let us talk.
