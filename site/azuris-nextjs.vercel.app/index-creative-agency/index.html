About the Platform

One national system for Startup Designation

StartupET replaces scattered paperwork with a single official channel, established under Proclamation No. 1396/2025 and operated by the Ministry of Innovation and Technology. Register your business, apply for Startup Designation, and track your certificate — all in one place.

Who Can Apply

Four ways to qualify

Designation is open to any Ethiopian-registered business that creates positive economic value through a highly scalable, tech-enabled product or model — via one of four innovation pathways.

Pathway 01

Technology Introduction

Bringing a genuinely new technology product or service to the Ethiopian market for the first time.

Pathway 02

Technology Adoption

Applying existing technology in a new way to solve a systemic local challenge.

Pathway 03

Technology Transplantation

Adapting a proven global model to fit Ethiopia's context and users.

Pathway 04

Reverse Engineering

Deconstructing and rebuilding technology locally to reduce import dependence.

Why Get Designated

What Startup Designation unlocks

Designation is more than a certificate — it opens practical, government-backed support built for early-stage, tech-enabled companies.

Credit Guarantee

A national scheme that lowers the collateral barrier to early-stage financing.

Procurement Preference

Preferential evaluation when bidding for government contracts (B2G).

Tax Relief

Tax holidays, capital equipment exemptions, and reduced corporate income tax.

Regulatory Sandbox

Test regulated products live before pursuing a full license.

Governance & Trust

Independent, expert evaluation at every stage

Startup Designation is governed through three clear tiers, so no single office decides alone.

Tier 1 · Policy

National Digital Economy Council

Sets the strategic direction for Ethiopia's startup and innovation policy.

Tier 2 · Execution

Ministry of Innovation and Technology

Operates the StartupET platform and administers the Startup Proclamation nationwide.

Tier 3 · Evaluation

National Startup Designation Committee

Reviews applications, conducts interviews where needed, and issues certificates independently.

Every application is assessed against the same published criteria — the four innovation pathways and the scalability test — with decisions documented for accountability.

StartupET is the single national portal, established under Proclamation No. 1396/2025, for founders seeking official Startup Designation in Ethiopia.

Any Ethiopian-registered business creating scalable, tech-enabled value through one of the four innovation pathways can apply.

Most complete applications are reviewed within 30–45 days.

Certificates are valid for two years and renewable, up to a cumulative maximum of eight years.

Designated startups gain access to tax relief, credit guarantees, public procurement preference, and — where relevant — the regulatory sandbox.
